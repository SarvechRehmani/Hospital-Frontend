Crisis Text Line

If you are in crisis, text HOME to 741741 to connect with a trained crisis counselor for support.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

If you or someone you know is in crisis, call the Lifeline at 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255) for immediate assistance.
